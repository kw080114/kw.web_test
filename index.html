About

Apex Legends is a free-to-play online battle royale game developed by Respawn Entertainment. Players form squads of two or three and choose from a cast of unique characters known as "Legends", each with their own tactical abilities, passive perks, and ultimate powers.

Set in the universe of Titanfall, the game takes place in the Outlands — a lawless, remote cluster of planets where legends battle for fame, glory, and survival. The Apex Games, a brutal bloodsport, attracts competitors from all walks of life: ex-soldiers, rogue scientists, bounty hunters, and even sentient robots.

Each season introduces new content, including maps like World’s Edge and Storm Point, limited-time modes, new legends, weapons, and rich narrative developments revealed through in-game events and animated stories.

Apex Legends stands out for its fast-paced movement, team-based strategy, deep lore, and strong community of players and creators. Whether you're in it for competitive ranked play or story-rich immersion, the game continues to evolve with every drop.
